SCMA469 Actuarial Statistics

Chapter 2 Introduction to Stochastic Processes

The course will cover the probabilistic framework for stochastic models of real-world applications with emphasis on actuarial work. We will illustrate some practical actuarial problems for which we will develop mathematical models, tools and techniques for analysing and quantifying the uncertainty of the problems.
